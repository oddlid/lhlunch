How to build "archroot_2014-06-01.tar.xz":

Download archlinux-bootstrap-2014.06.01-x86_64.tar.gz from http://ftp.df.lth.se/pub/archlinux/iso/2014.06.01/ or another mirror.
Unpack to a temporary location. This will create the directory "root.x86_64/". Enter that directory.
Run: sudo tar -cpJf /path/to/save/archroot_2014-06-01.tar.xz .
You can now delete root.x86_64/

Update this as needed for dates and version numbers when new bootstrap images are released.
